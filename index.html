About Me

Dr. Jaspreet S. Saini is an accomplished bioinformatician and microbial genomics expert with extensive experience in metagenomics and metatranscriptomics. He holds a Ph.D. in Environmental Microbiology from the University of Geneva, supported by a Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship. Currently, he is a scientific collaborator at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) and a member of the NCCR microbial consortium in Switzerland.
